## Summary

This paper explores the ethical use of generative AI tools, specifically ChatGPT, in academic writing, providing guidelines to ensure integrity and quality in research outputs. Engineers should care because it outlines practical recommendations for integrating AI into writing processes while maintaining ethical standards.

## Key Contribution

**The paper presents a framework for the ethical use of generative AI in academic writing, emphasizing transparency and human oversight.**

## Problem

The need for clear guidance on how to ethically use generative AI tools in the academic writing process, particularly in healthcare simulation.

## Method

**Approach:** The method involves using ChatGPT to assist in various stages of academic writing while ensuring ethical standards are met. It emphasizes human oversight to mitigate risks such as plagiarism and inaccuracies.

**Algorithm:**

1. 1. Identify the writing task and gather existing content.
2. 2. Use ChatGPT to generate suggestions or rephrase existing text.
3. 3. Review and edit the AI-generated content to ensure it reflects the author's voice.
4. 4. Verify the accuracy of any references or claims made by the AI.
5. 5. Document the use of AI tools in the manuscript.

**Input:** Existing text, prompts for content generation, or specific writing tasks.

**Output:** Revised text, outlines, or summaries that maintain the author's original intent.

**Key Parameters:**

- `transparency: true`
- `human oversight: required`
- `ethical guidelines adherence: mandatory`

**Complexity:** not stated

## Benchmarks

**Tested on:** Literature on ethical AI use in academic writing, guidelines from academic publishers

**Results:**

- accuracy of references: 7% authentic and accurate
- plagiarism risk: high if unchecked

**Compared against:** Traditional academic writing processes without AI assistance

**Improvement:** Significant improvement in writing efficiency while maintaining ethical standards.

## Implementation Guide

**Data Structures:** Text documents, Prompts for AI interaction

**Dependencies:** ChatGPT API or similar generative AI tools

**Core Operation:**

```python
text = ChatGPT.generate(prompt); revised_text = human_review(text);
```

**Watch Out For:**

- Ensure all AI-generated content is thoroughly reviewed for accuracy.
- Be cautious of over-reliance on AI for critical thinking tasks.
- Document all uses of AI in the manuscript to maintain transparency.

## Use This When

- You need to enhance the clarity and structure of existing academic text.
- You want to brainstorm ideas or generate outlines for research papers.
- You are looking for assistance in translating academic content.

## Don't Use When

- You require original content without prior input or context.
- You need to analyze data or interpret results critically.
- You are concerned about the risk of plagiarism or inaccuracies.
