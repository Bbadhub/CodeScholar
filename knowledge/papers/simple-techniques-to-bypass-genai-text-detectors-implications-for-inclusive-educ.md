## Summary

This study investigates the effectiveness of six Generative AI (GenAI) text detectors against manipulated AI-generated content to assess their reliability in educational settings. Engineers should care because understanding the limitations of these detectors can inform the development of more robust systems for maintaining academic integrity.

## Key Contribution

**The study reveals significant reductions in the accuracy of GenAI text detectors when faced with simple adversarial techniques, highlighting their unreliability in educational contexts.**

## Problem

The rise of GenAI tools has made it easier for students to produce text that may evade detection by AI text detectors, raising concerns about academic integrity.

## Method

**Approach:** The method involves generating AI text samples using popular GenAI tools and then applying various adversarial techniques to manipulate these samples. The manipulated texts are then tested against multiple GenAI text detectors to evaluate their effectiveness in detecting AI-generated content.

**Algorithm:**

1. Generate initial text samples using GenAI tools (e.g., GPT-4, Claude 2, Bard).
2. Apply adversarial techniques to modify the generated text.
3. Test the modified samples against AI text detectors.
4. Record the detection accuracy of each detector.
5. Analyze the results to assess the impact of adversarial techniques on detection reliability.

**Input:** Text samples generated by GenAI tools, modified using adversarial techniques.

**Output:** Detection accuracy rates of AI text detectors against manipulated text samples.

**Key Parameters:**

- `number_of_samples: 805`
- `number_of_adversarial_techniques: 6`
- `number_of_GenAI_tools: 3`

**Complexity:** not stated

## Benchmarks

**Tested on:** 15 original AI-generated text samples, 89 modified samples using adversarial techniques

**Results:**

- detection accuracy: 17.4% reduction in accuracy when faced with adversarial techniques

**Compared against:** Six major GenAI text detectors

**Improvement:** Significant reduction in detection accuracy when adversarial techniques were applied.

**Watch Out For:**

- Monitor for updates in GenAI tools that may affect detection capabilities.
