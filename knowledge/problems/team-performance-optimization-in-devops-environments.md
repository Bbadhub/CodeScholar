# Problem: Team Performance Optimization in DevOps Environments

This problem involves enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of teams working in DevOps settings. It focuses on optimizing team structures and practices to improve software delivery and overall performance.

## You Have This Problem If

- You notice delays in software delivery timelines.
- Team members report feeling overwhelmed or unclear about their roles.
- There is a lack of collaboration between different teams.
- Performance metrics show inconsistent results across teams.
- You are receiving feedback indicating that team dynamics are affecting productivity.
