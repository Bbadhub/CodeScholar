# Problem: Hate Speech Detection and Ideological Extremism Classification

Hate speech detection involves identifying and classifying offensive or harmful language directed at individuals or groups based on attributes such as race, religion, or gender. Ideological extremism classification focuses on categorizing content that promotes radical beliefs or actions. Both problems are critical for maintaining safe online environments.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are developing a social media moderation tool.
- You need to filter user-generated content for harmful language.
- You are analyzing sentiment in online discussions.
- You are encountering challenges with false positives in content classification.
- You are working with large datasets of user comments or posts.
