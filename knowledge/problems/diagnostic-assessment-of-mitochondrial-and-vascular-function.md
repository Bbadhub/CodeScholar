# Problem: Diagnostic Assessment of Mitochondrial and Vascular Function

This problem involves evaluating the functionality of mitochondria and blood vessels to diagnose various health conditions. It is crucial for understanding cardiovascular health, metabolic disorders, and overall physiological performance.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are assessing patients for cardiovascular diseases or diabetes.
- You need to monitor metabolic health in athletes.
- You are evaluating microcirculatory dysfunction in chronic diseases.
