# Problem: Pharmacokinetics Analysis

Pharmacokinetics analysis involves studying how drugs are absorbed, distributed, metabolized, and excreted in the body. This analysis is crucial for optimizing drug dosing strategies and ensuring patient safety during treatment.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are designing new pain management protocols.
- You need to evaluate drug combinations in clinical trials.
- You are assessing the safety and efficacy of fixed-dose combination medications.
- You require a detailed understanding of drug behavior over time.
- You are working with patient-specific dosing regimens.

## Start Here

**Start with pharmacokinetic modeling as it provides a comprehensive framework for analyzing drug behavior and optimizing dosing strategies.**
