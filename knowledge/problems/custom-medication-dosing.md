# Problem: Custom Medication Dosing

Custom medication dosing involves creating personalized dosages of medication tailored to individual patient needs. This is particularly important for patients with specific health conditions or for pediatric patients who require precise dosing.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are working with patients who have unique medication requirements.
- You need to create personalized medication solutions.
- You are in a clinical setting that requires on-demand medication manufacturing.
- You are facing challenges with standard dosages not being effective for certain patients.
- You are exploring innovative manufacturing techniques for medication.

## Start Here

**Start with semisolid extrusion additive manufacturing as it provides a balance of customization and accuracy for medication dosing.**
