# Problem: Social Bot Detection

Social bot detection involves identifying automated accounts on social media that mimic human behavior. These bots can manipulate public opinion, spread misinformation, or engage in spammy activities, making their detection crucial for maintaining the integrity of online interactions.

## You Have This Problem If

- You notice unusual patterns of engagement on social media posts.
- There are accounts that post similar content at high frequencies.
- User interactions seem automated or lack genuine human responses.
- You observe accounts with little to no personal information or history.
- There is a sudden spike in followers or interactions that seem unnatural.
