# Problem: Predictive Modeling for Athlete Performance

Predictive modeling for athlete performance involves using data and statistical techniques to forecast an athlete's future performance based on various metrics. This can include physical, cognitive, and historical performance data to make informed decisions in sports management and recruitment.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are trying to assess the potential of athletes based on their past performances.
- You need to integrate cognitive assessments into your performance analytics.
- You are working with teams to evaluate player prospects.
- You have access to a variety of performance metrics and cognitive data.
- You are looking to improve decision-making in athlete recruitment.

## Start Here

**The recommended first approach for most cases is to use the Athletic Intelligence Quotient (AIQ) technique, as it effectively integrates cognitive assessments into predictive models, providing a unique perspective on athlete potential.**

## Approaches

### Athletic Intelligence Quotient (AIQ)

**Best for:** When developing predictive models for athlete performance based on cognitive assessments.

**Tradeoff:** While AIQ provides valuable insights into cognitive abilities, it may not capture all physical performance aspects.
