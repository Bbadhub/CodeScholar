# Problem: Workflow Management in Software Development

Workflow management in software development involves organizing and optimizing the processes that teams use to deliver software. It aims to enhance productivity, improve collaboration, and ensure timely project delivery.

## You Have This Problem If

- Your team struggles to meet project deadlines.
- You have multiple ongoing projects with overlapping tasks.
- Communication breakdowns are common in your team.
- You need better visibility into project progress.
- Your current project management approach feels rigid and unresponsive.

## Start Here

**Start with Kanban as it provides a visual workflow management system that can help your team adapt to changes and improve delivery timelines.**

## Decision Matrix

| Technique | Speed | Memory | Accuracy | Ease | Best When |
|-----------|-------|--------|----------|------|-----------|
| **Kanban** | medium | low | medium | easy | You need a visual representation of tasks and want to adapt to changes quickly. |

## Approaches

### Kanban

**Best for:** when you need to manage complex workflows in software development and require flexibility.

**Tradeoff:** While Kanban offers flexibility, it may lack the structured planning of other methodologies.
