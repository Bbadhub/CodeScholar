# Problem: System Reliability Analysis

System reliability analysis involves assessing the dependability of a system in terms of its ability to perform its required functions under stated conditions for a specified period. This is crucial for ensuring that systems can withstand failures and continue to operate effectively.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are experiencing frequent system failures.
- Your system's performance degrades under load.
- You need to ensure high availability for critical applications.
- You are implementing a new distributed system architecture.
- You are evaluating the reliability of a multi-component system.
