# Problem: Automated Irrigation System

An automated irrigation system is designed to manage the watering of plants without manual intervention. It aims to optimize water usage while ensuring that plants receive the necessary moisture for healthy growth.

## You Have This Problem If

- You have indoor plants that require consistent watering.
- You are concerned about water waste in your irrigation practices.
- You want to monitor and control your irrigation system remotely.
- You are looking for a sustainable solution to manage plant care.
- You have limited time to manually water your plants.
