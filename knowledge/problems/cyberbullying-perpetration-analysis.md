# Problem: Cyberbullying Perpetration Analysis

Cyberbullying perpetration analysis involves understanding the factors that lead individuals, particularly adolescents, to engage in online aggression. This analysis is crucial for developing effective interventions and educational programs aimed at reducing cyberbullying incidents.

## You Have This Problem If

- You notice an increase in reports of online harassment among adolescents.
- You are tasked with developing educational programs about online behavior.
- You are analyzing psychological factors related to aggressive online behavior.
- You are involved in creating interventions to mitigate cyberbullying.

## Start Here

**The moderated mediation model is the recommended first approach for analyzing cyberbullying perpetration due to its ability to uncover complex relationships between psychological factors and online aggression.**
