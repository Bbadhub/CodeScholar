# Problem: Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis is the process of determining the emotional tone behind a series of words, used to understand the attitudes, opinions, and emotions expressed in text. It is commonly applied in various fields such as finance, marketing, and customer service to gauge public sentiment and inform decision-making.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are analyzing customer feedback or reviews.
- You need to assess social media sentiment about a brand.
- You are working with investor communications.
- You are developing chatbots that require understanding of indirect language.
- You want to automate decision-making processes based on text data.
