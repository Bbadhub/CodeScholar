# Problem: Network Formation Time Optimization

Network formation time optimization involves reducing the time it takes for a network to establish connections and become operational after disruptions. This is particularly important in environments like IoT and smart grids where downtime can lead to significant inefficiencies and losses.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are experiencing delays in network recovery after outages.
- Your application requires quick re-establishment of connections.
- You are working with distributed systems that need efficient network formation.
- You notice performance degradation during network reconnections.
- Your system is sensitive to latency and requires rapid response times.
