# Problem: Nonlinear Modeling

Nonlinear modeling involves creating mathematical models that capture complex relationships between variables that cannot be accurately represented by linear equations. This is particularly important in fields like manufacturing and semiconductor performance, where relationships are often intricate and multifaceted.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are struggling to fit a linear model to your data.
- Your data exhibits significant curvature or nonlinearity.
- You need to improve the accuracy of predictions in your application.
- You have a dataset with complex interactions between variables.
- Existing models do not adequately capture the behavior of your system.
