# Problem: Interpersonal Musical Biofeedback

Interpersonal musical biofeedback involves using music to enhance communication and emotional connection between individuals by responding to their physiological signals. This approach can be particularly beneficial in therapeutic settings and interactive art installations.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are developing applications that use music for communication.
- You want to create therapeutic tools that respond to users' emotional states.
- You are designing interactive installations that engage audiences through music.
- You have access to physiological data from users.
- You are interested in the intersection of technology, music, and emotional well-being.
