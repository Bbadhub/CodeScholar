# Problem: Technology Adoption in Education

This problem involves the integration of new technologies into educational settings, focusing on how these technologies can enhance learning experiences. It encompasses the challenges of acceptance, implementation, and effective use of technology by educators and students.

## You Have This Problem If

- You notice resistance from educators towards new technologies.
- Students show a lack of engagement with digital tools.
- There is a gap between technology availability and its actual use in classrooms.
- Stakeholders express concerns about the effectiveness of new educational technologies.
- You are exploring innovative solutions to improve learning outcomes.

## Start Here
