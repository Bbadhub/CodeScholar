# Problem: Drug-Drug Interaction Detection

Drug-drug interaction detection involves identifying potential adverse interactions between different medications. This is crucial for ensuring patient safety and effective treatment plans in pharmacology.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are analyzing multiple medications simultaneously.
- You have encountered unexpected adverse reactions in patients.
- You are working on a pharmacovigilance project.
- You need to ensure high accuracy in your predictions.
- You are dealing with complex relationships in drug interactions.
