# Problem: Training Effectiveness Under Stress in Emergency Response Scenarios

This problem focuses on evaluating how effective training programs are for emergency responders when they are subjected to high-stress situations. It aims to identify the best methods to enhance learning and performance in critical scenarios.

- You need to assess cognitive load during training.
- You are using virtual reality (VR) for training simulations.
- You want to create adaptive training based on real-time data.
- You are interested in improving performance under stress.
