# Problem: Video Encryption

Video encryption is the process of converting video content into a secure format to prevent unauthorized access during transmission or storage. This is particularly important for sensitive video content that needs to be shared over insecure networks like the internet.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are transmitting sensitive video content.
- You need to protect video files from unauthorized access.
- You are working with cloud platforms for video storage.
- You require efficient playback of encrypted videos.
- You are dealing with large video files that need encryption.

## Start Here

**The recommended first approach for most cases is the Video Slice Encryption Scheme, as it effectively balances security and playback efficiency for sensitive video content.**
