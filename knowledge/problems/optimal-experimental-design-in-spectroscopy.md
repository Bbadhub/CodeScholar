# Problem: Optimal Experimental Design in Spectroscopy

Optimal experimental design in spectroscopy involves determining the best measurement conditions to obtain the most informative data from spectroscopic experiments. This is crucial for improving the efficiency and accuracy of material characterization processes.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are conducting spectroscopic experiments.
- You need to optimize measurement conditions.
- You want to automate data acquisition.
- You have access to a standard spectral database.
- You are facing challenges in data interpretation due to suboptimal experimental setups.
