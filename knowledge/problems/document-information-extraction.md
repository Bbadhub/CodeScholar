# Problem: Document Information Extraction

Document information extraction involves automatically retrieving structured data from unstructured documents such as invoices, receipts, and forms. This process is crucial for automating workflows and improving data accuracy in various applications.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are frequently processing invoices or receipts.
- You encounter various document formats that need data extraction.
- You require high accuracy in the extracted information.
- You have limited training data available for your extraction models.
- You need to reduce manual data entry efforts.

## Start Here

**Start with Applied Key Information Extraction (KIE) as it is specifically designed for high accuracy in extracting information from invoices and receipts, making it a suitable choice for your needs.**
