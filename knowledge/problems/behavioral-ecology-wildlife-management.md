# Problem: Behavioral Ecology and Wildlife Management

This problem involves understanding the interactions between wildlife and their environments, particularly in the context of managing wildlife populations and their behaviors. Effective management strategies are crucial for conservation efforts and agricultural practices.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are observing unusual wildlife behavior in agricultural areas.
- You need to balance wildlife conservation with agricultural productivity.
- You are studying predator-prey dynamics in a specific ecosystem.
- You are tasked with developing management strategies for wildlife populations.
- You are encountering conflicts between wildlife and livestock.
