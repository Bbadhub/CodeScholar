# Problem: Human-Robot Interaction

Human-robot interaction involves the ways in which humans communicate and collaborate with robots. This can include verbal communication, emotional responses, and physical interactions, particularly in social or therapeutic contexts.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are developing robots that need to interact with people in social settings.
- You require robots to respond to human emotions or commands.
- You are working on educational robots that need to engage with students.
- You are designing robots for therapeutic applications.
- You need to create robots that can operate in environments like homes or hospitals.

## Start Here

**The recommended first approach for most cases is the Sound-Based Reaction System, as it effectively addresses emotional engagement in social settings, which is crucial for human-robot interaction.**
