# Problem: Predictive Modeling for Cardiovascular Aging

Predictive modeling for cardiovascular aging involves using data to forecast the health trajectory of elderly patients, particularly in relation to cardiovascular health. This can help in early detection of diseases and understanding the impact of various health markers.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are working with elderly patient data.
- You need to predict health outcomes based on various health indicators.
- You are developing a healthcare application.
- You have access to immunological markers and other health data.
- You are looking for a robust classification model.
