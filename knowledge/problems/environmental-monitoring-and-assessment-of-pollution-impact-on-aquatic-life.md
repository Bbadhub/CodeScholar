# Problem: Environmental Monitoring of Aquatic Pollution

This problem involves assessing the impact of pollution, particularly microplastics, on aquatic ecosystems and the health of marine life. It aims to develop effective monitoring protocols to evaluate the extent of contamination and its effects on fish health.

## You Have This Problem If

- You observe declining fish populations in a specific area.
- Water quality tests indicate high levels of microplastics.
- There are noticeable changes in fish behavior or health.
- Local reports indicate increased pollution levels in nearby water bodies.
- You are developing a study to assess the ecological impact of pollutants.
