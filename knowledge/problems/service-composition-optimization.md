# Problem: Service Composition Optimization

Service composition optimization involves selecting and arranging services to achieve desired outcomes while considering various constraints and quality attributes. This problem is particularly relevant in environments like cloud manufacturing, where service states can be uncertain and dynamic.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are dealing with multiple services that need to be combined for a specific task.
- You notice performance issues due to suboptimal service arrangements.
- You have varying quality of service (QoS) attributes for different services.
- You require flexibility in your service composition to adapt to changing conditions.
- You are working in a cloud environment with a large repository of services.
