# Problem: Quality Assessment of Consumer Products

Quality assessment of consumer products involves evaluating the durability and overall performance of items to ensure they meet consumer expectations. This is particularly important in industries like clothing, where product longevity can significantly impact customer satisfaction and brand reputation.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are developing an e-commerce platform for clothing.
- You need to establish a quality assurance program for garments.
- You are conducting research on textile materials.
- You want to improve customer feedback on product quality.
- You are facing high return rates due to quality issues.
