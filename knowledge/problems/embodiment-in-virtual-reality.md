# Problem: Embodiment in Virtual Reality

Embodiment in virtual reality refers to how users perceive themselves within a virtual environment, often influenced by the representation of their avatars. This can significantly affect user experience, engagement, and the effectiveness of simulations or social interactions in VR.

## You Have This Problem If

- Users report feeling disconnected from their avatars.
- There are issues with user engagement in VR applications.
- Feedback indicates that users do not identify with their virtual representations.
- Training outcomes are suboptimal due to lack of immersion.
- Social interactions in VR feel awkward or biased.
