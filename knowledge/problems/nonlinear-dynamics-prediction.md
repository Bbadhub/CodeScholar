# Problem: Nonlinear Dynamics Prediction

Nonlinear dynamics prediction involves forecasting the behavior of systems where outputs are not directly proportional to inputs, often leading to complex and unpredictable behavior. This is particularly relevant in fields like plasma physics, where understanding instabilities can significantly impact system performance.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are working with systems that exhibit chaotic behavior.
- You have limited data from simulations or experiments.
- You need to make predictions in real-time for control systems.
- Your models are struggling to capture the complexity of the dynamics.
- You are dealing with high-dimensional data that is difficult to analyze.
