# Problem: State of Charge Estimation

State of charge (SoC) estimation is crucial for battery management systems, as it determines the current energy level of a battery. Accurate SoC estimation helps in optimizing battery performance, ensuring safety, and prolonging battery life.

## You Have This Problem If

- You need to monitor battery performance in real-time.
- You are developing applications that rely on battery longevity.
- You encounter issues with battery safety and reliability.
- You require accurate readings under varying environmental conditions.
- You are working with advanced battery technologies like solid-state batteries.

## Start Here

**The recommended first approach for most cases is to use EIS-based SoC estimation due to its high accuracy, especially in applications involving solid-state batteries and safety-critical systems.**

## Approaches

### Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy (EIS) based SoC estimation

**Best for:** when developing battery management systems for solid-state batteries or safety-critical applications in aerospace.

**Tradeoff:** Provides high accuracy but may require complex setup and calibration.
