# Problem: Market Prediction and Consumer Behavior Analysis

This problem involves predicting market trends and understanding consumer behavior, often influenced by various factors including social media and marketing strategies. Engineers face challenges in accurately forecasting outcomes based on historical data and current trends.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are analyzing sales data to predict future trends.
- You are evaluating the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
- You notice discrepancies between predicted and actual consumer behavior.
- You are using social media metrics to inform business decisions.
- You are developing models to assess the impact of influencers on market performance.
