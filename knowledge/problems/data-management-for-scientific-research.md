# Problem: Data Management for Scientific Research

Data management in scientific research involves organizing, storing, and sharing large datasets generated from experiments. Effective data management is crucial for ensuring compliance with data sharing principles and facilitating collaboration among researchers.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are dealing with large datasets from scientific experiments.
- You need to ensure compliance with FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) principles.
- You are collaborating with multiple researchers or institutions.
- You are facing challenges in data organization and retrieval.
- You require a systematic approach to data sharing and management.

## Start Here
