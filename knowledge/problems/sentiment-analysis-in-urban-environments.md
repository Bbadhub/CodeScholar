# Problem: Sentiment Analysis in Urban Environments

Sentiment analysis in urban environments involves understanding public feelings and opinions about urban spaces, often derived from social media data. This analysis can help urban planners and designers make informed decisions based on the emotional responses of the community.

## You Have This Problem If

- You have access to social media data related to urban spaces.
- You are interested in understanding public sentiment towards specific urban features.
- You are involved in urban planning or design projects.
- You need to correlate sentiment with urban design elements.
- You are looking for data-driven insights to improve urban environments.
