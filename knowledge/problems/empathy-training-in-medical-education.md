# Problem: Empathy Training in Medical Education

Empathy training in medical education focuses on enhancing the ability of healthcare professionals to understand and share the feelings of their patients. This is crucial for improving patient care and communication, especially in complex clinical scenarios.

## You Have This Problem If

- Medical students struggle to connect with patients emotionally.
- There is a noticeable lack of patient-centered communication in clinical settings.
- Feedback from patients indicates a feeling of being misunderstood or neglected.
- Students express difficulty in relating to patients with chronic pain.
- Curriculum assessments show low scores in empathy-related competencies.

## Start Here

**The recommended first approach is the Narrative-driven Virtual Reality Experience, as it offers an innovative way to engage students and enhance their empathetic skills through realistic scenarios.**
