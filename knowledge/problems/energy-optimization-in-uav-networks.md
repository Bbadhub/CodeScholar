# Problem: Energy Optimization in UAV Networks

Energy optimization in UAV networks involves finding ways to reduce energy consumption while maintaining performance and reliability. This is particularly important for UAVs operating in environments where battery life is limited and efficient energy use is critical for mission success.

- You need to optimize microservice execution for battery-operated devices.
- You are designing resilient communication networks in rural areas.
- You are facing challenges with UAV battery life affecting operational range.
- You are looking for ways to improve the energy efficiency of UAV communication protocols.
