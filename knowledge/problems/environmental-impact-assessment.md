# Problem: Environmental Impact Assessment

Environmental impact assessment involves evaluating the potential effects of a project or technology on the environment. This process is crucial for ensuring that new developments do not harm ecological systems and comply with regulations.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are assessing the environmental effects of a new technology.
- You need to comply with environmental regulations for a project.
- You are formulating policies related to technology deployment.
- You are conducting a study that requires statistical analysis of environmental data.
