# Problem: Optical Combiner Design for Augmented Reality

The design of optical combiners is crucial for augmented reality (AR) applications, as they enable the integration of virtual images with the real world. This involves creating systems that can dynamically adjust to provide clear and effective visual overlays for users.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are developing XR applications that require dynamic visual overlays.
- You need lightweight and adaptable optical systems for head-mounted displays.
- You aim to improve user experience in augmented reality environments.
