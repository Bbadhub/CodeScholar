# Problem: Load Management in Electrical Distribution Networks

Load management in electrical distribution networks involves optimizing the distribution of electrical loads to ensure efficiency and reliability. This is particularly important with the increasing adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) and the need for smart grid solutions.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are experiencing frequent overloads in your electrical distribution system.
- You are integrating electric vehicle charging stations into your network.
- You notice inefficiencies in energy distribution during peak hours.
- You are tasked with designing a smart grid solution.
- You need to evaluate the impact of new technologies on local power distribution.
