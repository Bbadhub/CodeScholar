# Problem: Sentiment Classification

Sentiment classification involves determining the emotional tone behind a body of text. This is commonly used to analyze user opinions, reviews, and social media content to understand public sentiment.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are analyzing customer reviews or feedback.
- You need to gauge public opinion on social media.
- You are working with large datasets of text.
- You require insights into user-generated content.
- You want to automate the process of sentiment analysis.
