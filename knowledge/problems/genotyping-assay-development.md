# Problem: Genotyping Assay Development

Genotyping assay development involves creating methods to analyze genetic variations within organisms. This is crucial for applications such as plant breeding, where understanding genetic traits can enhance selection and efficiency.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are working on improving breeding efficiency in crops.
- You need to track genetic traits during the early selection process.
- You are looking for a cost-effective solution for genotyping.
- You require reliable data on genetic variations.
- You are involved in research or development in agricultural biotechnology.
