# Problem: Supply Chain Risk Modeling

Supply chain risk modeling involves identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks that can disrupt the flow of goods and services. It is crucial for organizations to understand potential vulnerabilities in their supply chains to enhance resilience and ensure continuity.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are experiencing frequent supply chain disruptions.
- You have difficulty assessing the impact of risks on your supply chain.
- You need to analyze historical data to predict future risks.
- You want to improve your risk management strategies.
- You are collaborating with multiple stakeholders in your supply chain.
