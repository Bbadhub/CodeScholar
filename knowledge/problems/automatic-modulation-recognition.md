# Problem: Automatic Modulation Recognition

Automatic modulation recognition involves identifying the modulation scheme of a received signal in communication systems. This is crucial for efficient spectrum management and improving communication reliability in crowded environments.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are working with signals in a crowded electromagnetic spectrum.
- You need to classify different types of modulated signals.
- You are facing challenges with traditional classification methods.
- You require high accuracy in signal classification.
- You want to leverage both spatial and temporal features in your analysis.
