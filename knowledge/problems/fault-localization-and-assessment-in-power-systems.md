# Problem: Fault Localization and Assessment in Power Systems

Fault localization and assessment in power systems involves identifying and evaluating defects in electrical infrastructure, particularly in high-voltage power cables. Accurate detection is crucial to ensure reliability and safety in power distribution.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are experiencing unexplained outages in your power system.
- Traditional fault detection methods are yielding inconclusive results.
- You suspect there are subtle defects in high-voltage cables.
- You need to assess the severity of faults accurately.
- You are dealing with complex fault types that are hard to diagnose.
