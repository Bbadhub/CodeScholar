# Problem: Lead Qualification

Lead qualification is the process of determining which potential customers are most likely to convert into paying customers. This involves analyzing historical data and applying models to prioritize leads based on their likelihood of engagement and purchase.

## You Have This Problem If

- You have a large volume of leads to manage.
- Your sales team is spending too much time on unqualified leads.
- You have historical data on lead interactions and conversions.
- You want to improve the efficiency of your sales process.
- You are looking to implement automation in your CRM system.

## Start Here

**The recommended first approach is to implement the Lead Qualification Model, as it leverages historical data to automate and improve lead management effectively.**
