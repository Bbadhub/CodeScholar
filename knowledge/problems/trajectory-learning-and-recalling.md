# Problem: Trajectory Learning and Recalling

Trajectory learning and recalling involves understanding and predicting the paths taken by agents in a given environment. This problem is crucial for developing AI systems that can navigate and make decisions based on past experiences.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are building AI systems that require understanding of movement patterns.
- You need to develop models that mimic human memory processes.
- You are creating systems for navigation tasks that require recalling past trajectories.
- You are working with data that involves sequences of actions or movements.
- You are facing challenges in accurately predicting future states based on historical data.
