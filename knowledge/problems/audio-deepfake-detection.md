# Problem: Audio Deepfake Detection

Audio deepfake detection involves identifying manipulated audio recordings that may mislead listeners or misrepresent the speaker. This problem is critical in fields such as cybersecurity and journalism, where audio authenticity is paramount.

## You Have This Problem If

- You encounter suspicious audio recordings.
- You need to verify the authenticity of audio content.
- You are working in a domain where audio manipulation is a concern.
- You require a solution that can handle diverse audio inputs.
- You are facing challenges with existing audio verification methods.
