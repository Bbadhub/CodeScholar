# Problem: Interoceptive Prediction Error Analysis

Interoceptive prediction error analysis focuses on understanding how the brain processes internal bodily signals and how discrepancies between expected and actual interoceptive signals can affect mental and physical health. This analysis is particularly relevant in diagnosing and treating conditions like migraines and chronic pain.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are developing diagnostic tools for migraine based on interoceptive processing.
- You are creating therapeutic interventions targeting interoceptive abnormalities.
- You are researching the relationship between chronic pain and cognitive processes.
- You notice discrepancies between expected and actual bodily sensations.
- You are studying the impact of interoceptive awareness on mental health.
