# Problem: Trajectory Planning

Trajectory planning involves determining the optimal path for a moving object, such as a robotic arm or an autonomous vehicle, to follow in a given environment. This problem is critical in robotics and autonomous systems to ensure efficient and safe movement.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are working on robotic systems that require precise movement.
- Your application involves multiple agents interacting in a shared space.
- You need to minimize energy consumption and wear on mechanical components.
- Real-time decision-making is essential for your system's performance.
- You are dealing with complex environments that require adaptive solutions.

## Start Here

**Begin with the Improved Slime Mould Algorithm (ISMA) for most industrial applications, as it balances adaptability and optimization effectively.**

## Approaches

### Improved Slime Mould Algorithm (ISMA)

**Best for:** when you need to optimize robotic arm movements in industrial applications.

**Tradeoff:** Offers adaptability to complex environments but may require more tuning for specific applications.
