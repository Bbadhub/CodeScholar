# Problem: Nutritional Enhancement of Animal Feed

This problem involves improving the nutritional quality of animal feed to enhance growth and health in livestock. It often requires innovative techniques to utilize lower-grade ingredients effectively while ensuring the feed remains cost-effective.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are looking to improve the digestibility of animal feed.
- You want to enhance the nutritional profile of your livestock's diet.
- You are facing high feed costs and need to use lower-grade ingredients.
- You are interested in sustainable feeding practices.
- You have observed suboptimal growth rates in your animals.
