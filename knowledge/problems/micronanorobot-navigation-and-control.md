# Problem: Micro/Nanorobot Navigation and Control

Micro and nanorobot navigation and control involves the development of systems that enable precise movement and operation of tiny robots, often for medical applications. This problem is critical for tasks such as targeted drug delivery, minimally invasive surgeries, and advanced diagnostics.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are working on medical robotics applications.
- You need to develop intuitive control interfaces.
- You are implementing real-time navigation for small-scale robots.
- You require gesture-based control mechanisms.
- You are facing challenges in the precise movement of micro/nanorobots.

## Start Here

**The recommended first approach for most cases is to implement the Fully Integrated Wearable Control System (FIWCS) due to its focus on intuitive control and real-time navigation, which are crucial for effective micro/nanorobot operation.**
