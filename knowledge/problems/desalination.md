# Problem: Desalination

Desalination is the process of removing salt and other impurities from seawater or brackish water to produce fresh water. This is crucial for providing potable water in areas with limited freshwater resources.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are in a region with limited access to freshwater.
- You are involved in water purification projects.
- You are exploring innovative technologies for chemical separations.
- You need to meet specific water quality standards.
- You are facing challenges with existing desalination methods.
