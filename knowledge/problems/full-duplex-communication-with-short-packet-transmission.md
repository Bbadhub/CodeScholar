# Problem: Full-Duplex Communication with Short Packet Transmission

Full-duplex communication allows simultaneous transmission and reception of data, which is crucial for efficient wireless communication. This problem arises particularly in scenarios where short packets are frequently sent, requiring effective management of self-interference and spectral efficiency.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are experiencing delays in data transmission.
- Your system struggles with self-interference during communication.
- You need to optimize the use of available bandwidth.
- You are working with devices that frequently send small amounts of data.
- Your application requires real-time data exchange.
