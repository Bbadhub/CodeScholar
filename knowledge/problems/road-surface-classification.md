# Problem: Road Surface Classification

Road surface classification involves identifying the type of surface on which a vehicle is traveling, such as asphalt, gravel, or dirt. This classification is crucial for developing advanced driving assistance systems that enhance safety and navigation for vehicles, particularly motorcycles.

- You are working on an advanced driving assistance system.
- Your application requires real-time road surface recognition.
- You need to enhance navigation based on varying road conditions.
- You are developing features specifically for electric motorcycles.
