# Problem: Personalized Itinerary Recommendation

This problem involves creating tailored travel itineraries for individuals based on their unique preferences and interests. It aims to enhance the travel experience by suggesting points of interest (POIs) that align with the user's desires and available time.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are developing a travel application.
- Users express the need for customized travel plans.
- You have access to user preference data.
- You want to improve user engagement through personalized suggestions.
- You are considering integrating social media insights into your recommendations.
