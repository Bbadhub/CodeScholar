# Problem: Real-time Peak Segmentation in X-ray Crystallography

Real-time peak segmentation involves identifying and analyzing peaks in X-ray diffraction data to determine the structure of crystalline materials. This process is crucial for researchers in materials science and chemistry, as it allows for the rapid analysis of large datasets generated by X-ray experiments.
