# Problem: Control of Nonlinear Distributed Systems

Controlling nonlinear distributed systems involves managing systems where the dynamics are governed by nonlinear equations and the system is distributed across multiple locations. This can be particularly challenging due to the complexity and high dimensionality of the systems involved.

- You are dealing with high-dimensional nonlinear systems.
- You have limited data for system modeling.
- You require interpretable models for safety-critical applications.
- You are facing high computational costs in simulations.
- You need to ensure stability and performance in control tasks.
