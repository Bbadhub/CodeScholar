# Problem: Pose Recognition

Pose recognition involves identifying and tracking human body positions and movements in real-time. This technology is widely used in applications such as fitness training, gaming, and augmented reality.

## You Have This Problem If

- You need to analyze human body movements.
- You are developing applications that require real-time feedback.
- You are working on fitness or yoga-related software.
- You need to integrate pose recognition into a virtual environment.
- You are looking for ways to enhance user interaction through body movements.
