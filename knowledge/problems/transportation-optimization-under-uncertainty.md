# Problem: Transportation Optimization Under Uncertainty

Transportation optimization under uncertainty involves finding the most efficient way to transport goods while accounting for uncertain variables such as demand, costs, and travel times. This problem is critical for logistics and supply chain management, where fluctuations can significantly impact overall performance.

- Your demand forecasts are uncertain.
