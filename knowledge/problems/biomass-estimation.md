# Problem: Biomass Estimation

Biomass estimation involves quantifying the amount of organic material in a given area, which is crucial for understanding ecosystem health and productivity. This problem is particularly relevant in ecological studies where accurate measurements can inform conservation and management practices.

## You Have This Problem If

- You need to assess the health of a grassland ecosystem.
- You are conducting research on vegetation biomass.
- You require detailed 3D models of plant structures.
- You are analyzing litter accumulation in a specific area.
