# Problem: Crash Simulation

Crash simulation involves modeling and analyzing the behavior of vehicles and barriers during a collision to improve safety designs. It helps engineers evaluate the effectiveness of safety features and barriers in real-world scenarios.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are working on vehicle safety design.
- You need to test the impact of different barrier designs.
- You require rapid iterations for design modifications.
- You are involved in regulatory compliance for safety standards.
