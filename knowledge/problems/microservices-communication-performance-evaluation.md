# Problem: Microservices Communication Performance Evaluation

This problem involves assessing the efficiency and effectiveness of communication methods between microservices in a distributed system. It aims to identify bottlenecks and optimize the interactions to enhance overall system performance.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are experiencing latency issues in your microservices interactions.
- Your system's throughput is lower than expected.
- You are considering changes to your microservices communication protocols.
- You need to scale your microservices architecture and want to ensure optimal communication.
- You have multiple communication methods and need to evaluate their performance.
