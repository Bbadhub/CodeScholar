# Problem: Climate Modeling and Prediction

Climate modeling and prediction involves creating simulations to understand and forecast climate behavior based on various data inputs. This problem is critical for addressing climate change and its impacts on ecosystems and human activities.

## You Have This Problem If

- You notice inconsistencies in climate data for certain regions.
- Your climate models lack historical context.
- You are interested in incorporating unconventional data sources into your climate predictions.
- You are facing challenges in predicting climate trends accurately.
- You need to improve the robustness of your climate models.

## Start Here

**Start with non-traditional data integration as it allows for a more comprehensive understanding of climate patterns and can significantly enhance model accuracy.**
