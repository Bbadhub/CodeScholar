# Problem: Automated Documentation in Healthcare

Automated documentation in healthcare refers to the use of technology to streamline the process of recording patient information and clinical notes. This problem is critical in high-pressure medical environments where accuracy and efficiency are paramount for patient care.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are experiencing delays in clinical documentation.
- Healthcare professionals are overwhelmed with paperwork.
- There are frequent errors in patient records.
- You are looking to integrate AI tools into existing healthcare workflows.
- You need to improve the efficiency of record-keeping in a busy medical setting.

## Start Here

**The recommended first approach for most cases is to implement Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) due to its ability to enhance both the speed and accuracy of documentation in healthcare settings.**

## Approaches

### Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

**Best for:** When automating documentation in high-pressure medical environments and improving accuracy and efficiency in clinical record-keeping.

**Tradeoff:** While RAG can enhance documentation speed and accuracy, it may require significant initial setup and training.
