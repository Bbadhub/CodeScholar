# Problem: Parameter Optimization for Energy Systems

This problem involves finding the best parameters to enhance the performance of energy systems, such as Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFCs). It is crucial for improving efficiency and reducing costs in energy production.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are working with energy systems that require performance tuning.
- Existing optimization methods are not producing satisfactory results.
- You have access to experimental data for training models.
- You need to balance multiple performance metrics.
- You are facing challenges in achieving optimal operational conditions.
