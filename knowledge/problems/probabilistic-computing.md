# Problem: Probabilistic Computing

Probabilistic computing involves using algorithms that incorporate randomness to solve problems that are inherently uncertain or incomplete. This approach is particularly useful in scenarios where traditional deterministic methods may fail to provide accurate results.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are dealing with uncertain or incomplete data.
- Your application requires high-speed computations.
- You need to quantify uncertainty in your results.
- You are working in fields like machine learning or data analysis.
- You encounter noisy data that affects your computations.
