# Problem: Domain Generalization for Person Re-Identification

Domain generalization in person re-identification (ReID) focuses on improving the ability of models to recognize individuals across different environments and conditions. This is particularly important in applications like security and surveillance, where consistent identification is crucial despite varying backgrounds and lighting.

## You Have This Problem If

- You are facing challenges with model performance in different environmental conditions.
- You have multiple datasets that vary significantly in terms of lighting and background.
- Your current ReID model struggles with generalization to unseen domains.
- You are working on a project that requires robust identification in real-world scenarios.
- You have observed a drop in accuracy when deploying your model in new environments.
