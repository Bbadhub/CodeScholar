# Problem: Process Modeling and Optimization

Process modeling and optimization involves creating representations of organizational workflows to improve efficiency and effectiveness. This can include identifying bottlenecks, optimizing resource allocation, and formalizing tasks to enhance decision-making.

- You need to formalize tasks for machine learning applications.
- You are dealing with complex workflows that require optimization.
- You want to improve the interpretability of your models.
- You have multiple stakeholders involved in process design.
